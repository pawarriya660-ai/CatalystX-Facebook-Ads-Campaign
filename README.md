# CatalystX - Facebook Ads Lead Generation Campaign

## Project Overview
This project demonstrates the planning and creation of a Facebook Lead Generation campaign using Meta Business Suite and Ads Manager.

CatalystX is an imaginary startup consulting company that helps entrepreneurs transform innovative ideas into successful businesses through mentorship, branding, digital marketing, and investor guidance.

## Campaign Objective
Generate qualified leads for a free startup consultation using Facebook Instant Forms.

## Business Name
CatalystX

## Target Audience
- Country: India
- Age: 22–40 Years
- Gender: All
- Interests:
  - Entrepreneurship
  - Startup India
  - Business Development
  - Small Business
  - Digital Marketing
  - Technology

## Campaign Budget
- Daily Budget: ₹500
- Campaign Duration: 15 Days
